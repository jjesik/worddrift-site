Privacy Policy

WordDrift does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app does not use analytics tools, advertising networks, or third-party SDKs. It does not access your location, camera, microphone, contacts, or files.

There are no user accounts or login systems. All gameplay takes place locally on your device and is entirely private.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at support@worddrift.app.
